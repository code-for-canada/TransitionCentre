    C4C Fellows at CAF TG

    Technology and design for the user-centred and effective military transition into civilian life.

    Introduction

    This page intends to be the main public information-only page for our project with CAF TG. A key principle that is promoted both by the Code for Canada and the Government of Canada is to promote working in the open as much as possible.

    Disclaimer

    Understandably, due to confidentiality and security reasons, we can not make the main parts of our fellowship project public. However, we decided to create this public documentation and prototypes page to show the part of the work we can make public. The information hosted here is either already public information or approved by CAF TG for public release. The C4C Transition Centre is just a prototype for showing what is possible from a UX perspective, test some ideas with users as part of the user-centred design and show the benefits of an agile process.

    Sprints

    Please click on one of the Sprint buttons to visually see how our prototype has evolved with each sprint.

    • Sprint 0

      The 3 days practice sprint was done as part of our Onboarding. The challenge was to improve the experience to find information regarding transition through retirement at the existing Canada.ca page based on some user research reports that were provided to us.

    • Sprint 1

      Member Dashboard & Home Page (Authenticated) - Design and prototype an authenticated personal portal home page that a member lands on after logging-in, which will act as the central portal for personal transition resources and tools.
